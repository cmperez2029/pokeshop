The Global PokéShop Mission

Founded in 2026, the Pokémon Shopping Center began as a small kiosk in New Clark City. Our mission has always been simple: to provide trainers with the highest quality companions and items to aid them in their journey to become a Pokémon Master. We believe every trainer deserves a loyal partner, whether it's a common Magikarp or a legendary entity from another dimension.
